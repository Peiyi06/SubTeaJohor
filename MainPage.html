Freshly handcrafted tapioca

We insist on making the Deerioca from scratch: making the dough, kneading and rolling the dough into small balls, this procedure creates better texture and aroma for the tapioca. Each bite is unique, satisfying the craving for the perfect taste.

堅持從麵團、拍打、手揉到成形，增加珍珠咀嚼時的香氣口感，食味不完美的形、不一致的口味是唇齒的渴望。

Stawberry tapioca pearls

Peak strawberries are mashed with just enough raw sugar to soften any tartness, letting the berries’ floral, almost vegetal flavor shine through. Delightfully bouncy, still-warm boba happily drink in this ruby-red nectar; top the whole thing off with a healthy glug of ice-cold milk for a refreshing summer treat.

高峰草莓仅用足够的原糖捣碎即可软化任何酸味，让浆果的花香和几乎植物的香气散发出来。 在这种红宝石色的花蜜中令人愉悦的弹力，仍然温暖的波巴酒； 用一小撮健康的冰镇牛奶将整个事情结束，为您提神醒脑。

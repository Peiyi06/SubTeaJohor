The Classic's :

Black Sugar Bubble Tea (H/C) --- RM 8.50


The classic bubble tea which mixes black sugar with pure black tea. Our tea is brewed with real tea leaves rather than tea powder to bless our customer's taste buds with the wonderful aroma of Black tea. With black sugar & whipped cream infused in it, Voila! Our delicious Black Sugar Bubble Tea is on your hands!

ps. You can choose between cold or hot tapioca pearls as per your liking! :)


Black Sugar Bubble Milk Tea (H/C) --- RM 8.50


It's the same perfect blend of our signature, the Black Sugar Bubble Tea. But this time, we added milk in it for our milk lovers out there! (We see ya fams). The wonderful taste of milk blends perfectly with our Black Tea, and our chewy chewy bobas... bet you wanna have a taste!

ps. You can choose between cold or hot tapioca pearls as per your liking! :)


Taro Milk Tea --- RM 9.50


Taro! Taro! Taro! Delicious Taro! (Not to be messed up with our good o' Ube [a.k.a. Yam]). People go crazy for its flavour. Some say it tastes nutty; Some say it tastes like cookies & cream; And we say:"Why don't you give it a try?". Our Taro Milk Tea is made out from sweet diced taro with a blend of natural-tasting raw cane sugar & ofcourse with chilly ice to help you beat off that summer madness.

ps. You can choose between Non-Dairy Creamer or Whole Cream Milk as per your liking! :)

Thai Milk Tea (H/C) --- RM 8.00


Sawadeekab! Our Thai Milk Tea is a deliciously creamy, mildly spiced, slightly-caffeinated, and refreshingly sweet drink made from tea, milk, and sugar. Thai Milk Tea is well known in Southeast Asia and it's served in many Thai food restaurants. Unlike regular black tea, our Thai Milk Tea is infused with star anise and cloves and sweetened with sugar or sweetened condensed milk. Now, you can enjoy this sweet and creamy orange-colored iced tea at SubTea Malaysia!
:)

Matcha Milk Tea --- RM9.00


Feeling anxious? Have a Matcha Milk Tea to help calm your sense! Our Matcha Milk Tea is a mix of the famous Matcha Latte but with an addition of chewy tapioca pearls in it. The powerful blend of Dark brown sugar, Matcha and Oat Milk gives out a calm refreshing taste for those who drank.

ps. It's Lactose free! :)

Our Specials :

Strawberry Milk Tea --- RM11.50

Milky, fruity, chewy, nice or naughty. Our beautiful Strawberry Milk Tea is not only beautiful on the outside, but on it's taste as well. SubTea made this wonderful beverage by blending fresh strawberries and mixing it with fresh milk. In addition with tri-color jelly as our toppings and freshly sliced strawberries as decorations. This photegenic drink is definitely gonna make your day!

Galaxy Lemon Tea --- RM13.00

Finally our signature -- Galaxy Lemon Tea is a lemon tea with a beautiful twist. We source our high-quality butterfly pea flower from the northern part of Thailand. By mixing the blue pea with our fine jasmine green tea and fresh lime juice, this drink gives the taste, the smell and the alluring natural colors just like a beautiful butterfly flying among the stars.
